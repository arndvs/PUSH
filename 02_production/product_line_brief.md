# PUSH Product Line Brief

### RISE Technologies — Product Architecture

_For production use and riseawake.com development_
_Version: 1.0_

---

## OVERVIEW

RISE Technologies does not sell one bed. It sells a product line — a base product with a clear upgrade path designed to extract maximum value from affluent customers. The NUDGE (first-gen, Karen's era) was a single configuration. It taught RISE the market. The PUSH taught RISE the margin.

The full product line lives on riseawake.com. In the film, only two tiers appear: the **PUSH** and the **PUSH+**. The audience reads the difference through a single character on the badge. Everything else — the seven-pillow stack, the silk sheets, the warm glow, the quieter hum — is what that `+` buys you.

---

## THE TIERS

### PUSH — The Base Model

_Dave's bed. The product name is the entry-level tier._

The PUSH is a genuinely good product. It raises, rolls, folds, self-makes, and gets you to work. It does exactly what the spec sheet promises and nothing more. This is what gets you in the door.

**What ships:**

| Component | Spec |
| --- | --- |
| Frame | Adjustable base, matte dark charcoal, cool blue undertone |
| Mattress | Standard medium-depth, plain white/off-white cover |
| PUSH pillow | One. Contains pneumatic reshaping node. Standard cotton pillowcase (owner provides their own or uses the included white) |
| Fitted sheet | One. Standard cotton (owner provides their own or uses the included navy) |
| Remote | The PUSH remote. One button. |
| Casters | Low-profile, recessed. Functional, slightly audible on hard floors |
| Self-making | One tensioning bar (fitted sheet only). One pneumatic node (one pillow only) |
| Badge | `RISE / The PUSH / Smart Adjustable Base` |

**What the self-making handles:** The fitted sheet (tensioning bar draws it taut, center outward) and the one PUSH pillow (pneumatic node plumps and reshapes it). That's it. A flat sheet, duvet, or additional pillows the owner adds are not touched by the mechanism. The PUSH makes *its* bed. It doesn't make yours.

**Sound profile:** Warm low hum, 42Hz baseline. Faint caster whisper on hard floors. Sheet tensioning is a faint continuous whisper. Pillow node is a slow, patient exhale. The sound of a machine that works well.

---

### PUSH+ — The Premium Model

_Marcus's bed. Same product, everything upgraded._

The PUSH+ is what happens when someone with taste and budget looks at the PUSH configurator page and keeps clicking "add." Same core engineering, refined in every dimension. Toyota vs. Lexus — same chassis, the Lexus drives quieter, the doors feel fuller, everything is more considered. People notice. Nobody says anything.

**What ships (in addition to everything in PUSH):**

| Component | Spec |
| --- | --- |
| Frame | Premium colorway options (warm gray standard for PUSH+; seasonal limited editions available) |
| PUSH pillows | Three. Each contains a pneumatic reshaping node. Premium silk/sateen pillowcases in coordinated colorway |
| Additional soft goods | Two Euro shams (structural backing, coordinated). Two accent pillows (complementary tone, decorative). Premium silk/sateen fitted sheet. Premium flat sheet. Duvet + cover (high-thread-count, properly weighted) |
| Enhanced self-making | Enhanced tensioning system handles fitted sheet + flat sheet + duvet. Three pneumatic nodes (one per PUSH pillow). Faster, quieter |
| Atmosphere Suite | Frame-integrated mood lighting (warm, dimmable). Spatial audio module. Ambient wake/sleep programs |
| Casters | Dampened, whisper-quiet. The difference between economy and business class luggage wheels |
| Badge | `RISE / The PUSH+ / Smart Adjustable Base` |

**What the enhanced self-making handles:** Fitted sheet, flat sheet, and duvet (enhanced tensioning system covers all three layers). All three PUSH pillows (one pneumatic node per pillow). Euro shams and accent pillows stay wherever the owner placed them — the mechanism handles the functional items, not the decorative ones. The Atmosphere Suite shifts from warm amber (sleep) to cool daylight (wake) as part of the morning sequence.

**Sound profile:** Same frequency architecture as the PUSH, refined. Lower, smoother motor hum — you feel it more than hear it. Dampened caster roll. Pneumatic exhale is barely there — a breath, not a mechanism. Sheet tensioning is nearly inaudible. Panel and strut movement is solid, muted, damped — the "door closing" feel. Nothing rattles. Nothing clicks. It just moves. The sound of a machine engineered to disappear.

---

### PUSH+ Select — The Website Tier

_Nobody in the film owns this. It exists on riseawake.com._

The middle tier. It exists to make the pricing page work — the "Most Popular" badge tier with the best margin. It sits between PUSH and PUSH+ to make the PUSH+ feel like "only $X more" than the rational choice.

**What ships (in addition to everything in PUSH):**

| Component | Spec |
| --- | --- |
| Frame | Standard or premium colorway options |
| PUSH pillows | Two. Each contains a pneumatic reshaping node. Premium cotton pillowcases |
| Enhanced self-making | Enhanced tensioning (fitted sheet + flat sheet). Two pneumatic nodes. Quieter motor |
| Additional soft goods | Premium cotton sheet set. No duvet included (available as add-on) |
| Atmosphere Suite | Not included (available as add-on) |
| Badge | `RISE / The PUSH+ Select / Smart Adjustable Base` |

This tier's job is psychological. It anchors the PUSH+ as the "obvious upgrade" while making the base PUSH feel like a deliberate choice for people who "just want the basics."

## THE BADGE

The badge is the single visual detail that communicates the entire product line without dialogue.

**Dave's bed (PUSH):**

```
RISE
The PUSH
Smart Adjustable Base
```

**Marcus's bed (PUSH+):**

```
RISE
The PUSH+
Smart Adjustable Base
```

Same position (lower-right front face). Same layout. Same typeface. One character different. The audience reads it the way you read the badge on a car in the lane next to you. You just know.

**In the dual-bed office scene:** Two beds, side by side. Both badges visible. Both stickers at knee height in STATE THREE. The `+` is small, quiet, exactly the kind of detail that tells you everything about the person who chose it.

## THE SOUND COMPARISON

| Quality | PUSH (Dave) | PUSH+ (Marcus) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Motor hum | Low, present, slightly industrial | Lower, smoother, almost silent — you feel it more than hear it |
| Caster roll | Audible on hard floors, faint rattle | Dampened, whisper-quiet |
| Pneumatic exhale | Soft but clearly mechanical — you hear the air | Barely there — a breath, not a mechanism |
| Sheet tensioning | Faint continuous whisper | Nearly inaudible — the sheet just smooths |
| Panel/strut movement | Functional clicks and shifts | Solid, muted, damped — nothing rattles, nothing clicks, it just moves |
| Overall presence | A machine that works well | A machine engineered to disappear |

**In the office:** Dave's bed hums. Marcus's bed purrs. Same frequency range, different texture entirely. The audience may not consciously register why one sounds "better" — but they feel it.

**The dual hum (amended):** When both beds are in frame, their hums are slightly different — not just in pitch and sync, but in *quality*. Dave's has that faint industrial edge. Marcus's is smoother, more refined. The chord they create together is the sound of two men who made the same bad decision at wildly different price points.

## DAVE'S FULL BEDDING (PUSH BASE)

Everything Dave sleeps on, in order. The PUSH shipped with the frame, mattress, one pillow, and a navy fitted sheet. Everything else is Dave's own — cheap, functional, the kind of thing you grab at Target because you need *something* on the bed.

| Item | Source | Spec | Character |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fitted sheet | PUSH standard (or his own, doesn't matter — it's basic) | Navy, slightly faded from washing | Has been washed many times |
| Flat sheet | Dave's own | White, usually untucked or balled at the foot | Good intentions, imperfect execution |
| PUSH pillow | PUSH hardware | Standard size, slightly compressed, white pillowcase | The product |
| Personal pillow | Dave's own | Flat, generic white, from college. The kind you get at Target and never replace | The second pillow tells you everything |
| Duvet | None | — | He never bought one. The flat sheet is enough. Apparently. |
| Throw | None | — | — |
| Decorative anything | None | — | — |

**Self-making behavior:** The tensioning bar makes the fitted sheet taut. The pneumatic node plumps the PUSH pillow. Dave's flat sheet stays wherever he kicked it — usually balled at the foot of the bed. His college pillow sits undisturbed — flat, compressed, unremarkable. The PUSH makes what it can. It doesn't judge what it can't.

## MARCUS'S FULL BEDDING (PUSH+)

Everything Marcus sleeps on, in order. The PUSH+ shipped with the frame, mattress, three PUSH pillows, premium sheet set, duvet, Euro shams, accent pillows, and the Atmosphere Suite. Everything on Marcus's bed is PUSH product. He bought the full package. His bed is his calling card.

| Item | Source | Spec | Character |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fitted sheet | PUSH+ premium | Olive silk/sateen, coordinated | Deliberate color choice |
| Flat sheet | PUSH+ premium | Coordinated warm tone, properly tucked | He thought about this |
| Duvet | PUSH+ premium | Rich olive/warm tone, fuller, properly weighted | More volume, more quality |
| Duvet cover | PUSH+ premium | Coordinated, properly cornered | Marcus gets the corners in |
| PUSH pillow (center) | PUSH+ hardware | Premium, warm-tone silk pillowcase, pneumatic node | The product (same node, better pillowcase) |
| PUSH pillows (flanking) | PUSH+ hardware | Two matched, premium, warm-tone silk pillowcases, pneumatic nodes | Three nodes total |
| Euro shams | PUSH+ included | Two, deep olive, structural backing | Someone who owns Euro shams |
| Accent pillows | PUSH+ included | Two, complementary warm tone | Personality layer |
| Throw | PUSH+ included (or optional) | At foot, warm tone | Color break |

**Self-making behavior:** The enhanced tensioning system makes the fitted sheet, flat sheet, and duvet taut — all three layers handled. Three pneumatic nodes plump all three PUSH pillows. The Atmosphere Suite shifts from warm amber to cool daylight as part of the wake sequence. Euro shams and accent pillows stay exactly where Marcus placed them — which, because Marcus is Marcus, already looks perfect. The machine handles function. Marcus handles taste.

**Marcus's bedroom:** The PUSH+ isn't just on the bed. The Atmosphere Suite integrates with the room. Mood lighting — warm, dimmable, frame-integrated. Spatial audio module. The bedroom is curated to impress. Marcus is a ladies' man who takes his bedroom's appearance seriously, knowing it gives the girls he invites back the right vibe. His PUSH+ is a status symbol people recognize — he has the top-of-the-line model and people notice.

## KAREN'S NUDGE — First Generation, Retired

One configuration. No tiers. No upsells. That's part of why RISE rebranded to PUSH — the NUDGE taught them the market. The PUSH taught them the margin.

Karen's NUDGE: crisp white everything. Two personal pillows (same, centered, not PUSH hardware — the NUDGE may not have had the pneumatic system). Hotel-standard. The bed of someone who didn't need the bed to make itself, because she was already that person.

_"I had the NUDGE. Made me a VP."_ She sounds like a founding member of something.

## RISEAWAKE.COM IMPLICATIONS

The website is where the full product line comes alive. The film shows two tiers through two badges. The website fills in everything else.

### Product Comparison Page
Three-tier pricing grid. Feature checkmarks stacking toward the right. The "Most Popular" badge on PUSH+ Select. The PUSH+ priced as "only $X more" than Select. The base PUSH with suspiciously few checkmarks.

### Build Your PUSH Configurator
Running price total that keeps climbing. "Most customers also add:" with social proof percentages. Every click feels reasonable in isolation. $12,000 later, your bed has mood lighting.

### Scarcity & Exclusivity
- "Only 3 left in Warm Gray"
- "Ships in 6-8 weeks" for premium colorways
- "Founding Member" pricing for early adopters
- Waitlists for seasonal limited editions

### PUSH Renew — Subscription
$29/mo. Fresh pillowcases and fitted sheet quarterly. "Because your sleep deserves consistency." Adds comedic depth to the satirical website without needing to appear in any production doc.

### Social Proof & Testimonials
Subtly absurd: "My PUSH+ changed my mornings. And my evenings. And my Saturdays." Escalating in conviction by tier.

### The "Why Upgrade?" Page
Side-by-side before/after of a PUSH vs. PUSH+. Styled like a luxury car comparison. The visual language of status made explicit.
